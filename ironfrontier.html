ABOUT THE GAMEDOC.01

The Iron Frontier is a narrative-driven experience set in an alternate history where the space race not only didn't end, but was far more succesful resulting in humanity expanding into the solar system. Humanity would land on the Moon in 1965, setting the first woman on the Moon, and the Soviets beating the US in the race to the moon. Deapite this, the race was not over and now both sides would race to establish their lunar bases, orbital stations and ventures onward to both Venus and Mars.

The Iron Frontier takes place in 1989, in this alternate history. Yoh play as a Cosmonaut on the surface of Venus designing the next major project. The Venetian Mass Driver Rail, or the V.M.D.R. After a months long outage due to weather, communications finally return only for you and your partner to discover the Floating Islands of Venus are entirely vacant, along with it a pending message awaiting you. Unable to reach you, the evacuation notice told everyone else to leave, whilst both of you were stuck on the surface.
